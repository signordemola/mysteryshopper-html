AIM “Yet, we knew of secret shopper companies, so we hatched out an alternative strategy: tap the secret shoppers themselves for our research. In other words, we would ‘shop the shoppers.’” Nowakowski explains that Business Evaluation Services was the only one of the three shopper companies they vetted that took them up on that strategy (Nowakowski says the others wouldn’t even consider it). It turned out to be the best experience he could have imagined for his client’s project. They created a painless experience for us to satisfy our client’s needs. That’s why for flexibility in the age of COVID-19, Business Evaluation Services would be the one to tap not only for gaining knowledge about products and behaviors in traditional secret shopper mode, but also in hatching unique strategies that help you arrive at the truth around human behavior and products.”

Jim Nowakowsk
President
